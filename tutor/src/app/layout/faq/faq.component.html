4. Promotion/Selling/Coupons/Marketing

  1. How can I promote my course?

    Hover your mouse pointer over Marketing tab. Bulk SMS and bulk E-mails are two ways you can promote your courses. Select the channel, students and details and click on send.

  2. How can I generate coupons and send it?

    Hover your mouse pointer over Marketing and click on Manage coupons. Create coupons and the discount you want to provide it and send it to students via bulk SMSs and bulk e-mails.

  3. Rules to price a course?

    Hover the mouse pointer over Courses and click on Add Subject. Enter the Subject details and check Paid option, select gateway, amount and its validity.

5. Course management

  1. How to add/edit a course, subject and topic and its name?

    Hover the mouse pointer over Courses. There you will see Add Subjects, Add Topic, Create course content options. Once you click on any of these options, you will see the sub headings. You need to add the respective details there and click on Submit.

  2. How to add/edit price to a course/test?

    Hover on Courses and click on Add Topics and select the Subject. Once you select the subject, you can add a new topic at the end of the page or change the price of any selected topic.

  3. How can I add/remove:
    1. PDFs
    2. PPTs
    3. Audio
    4. Videos

    Hover your mouse pointer over Courses and click on Create Course Content. You will be able to select the Subject and the Topic, once you select these, you will see option to add the topic related document there. You will also be able to see the existing documents. You can delete the unrequired documents.

  4. How can I add practice and assessment questions?

    Under Test Series, you can see 2 options Test Questions and Practice Questions. Click the one you need to add. Select the Subject and Topic and the Instructions and other details. You can download a sample questions in Excel format. You need to create your questions and the answers in the same format. Once you have done it, upload it by clicking on Choose File. The questions will be live for the topic you have chosen.
